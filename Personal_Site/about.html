For me, a healthy work-life balance helps me be a great collaborator and best serve the needs of those I am working with. Here are some of the ways I achieve that balance.

Golfing Golfing helps me clear my mind when I feel it becoming a bit cloudy. Golf has served as an escape and stress-management tool in my life and I am so glad to have found it.

I found golf in my college years when I started playing with a friend. It was something I never thought I would be interested in. It wasn't until I started seeing all of the results of my practice and hard work to improve that I fell in love with the game.

Golf teaches me to be patient and stick with it through the end, which I take into my work and personal life as well. It is a very hard game to learn and there are a lot of downs when you can't figure out a certain swing or dial in a certain club. However, once I finally see results I can't help but feel an overwhelming joy and release of tension and that is the feeling I chase when I play golf. TV/Movies Another escape I have found to de-stress and clear my mind is TV and movies. I value the creativity and storytelling that goes into creating TV and movies and I love to get lost in it from time to time.

Though watching television does not necessarily bring out my own creativity, I do feel that it helps me learn about other people's versions of creativity and stories that I may then be abel to pull from later in my own creative ventures.

All in all, I enjoy the escape of television so much and am always stuck on a new TV show or raving about the last movie I watched. It is something that bonded my family and I early on in life and I'm sure I will be unapologetically recapping full TV shows to my peers for long time to come. My Dog, Belly My favorite way to create a healthy work-life balance is spending time with my dog, Belly. He is a 4 pound Chihuahua mix and he makes my days so much brighter than they were without him.

Having this little guy to come home to each day can make the worst days better. He helps me to reset and begin each day with a fresh, new perspective.

I can't say enough how grateful I am to Belly and the light he brings to my life!
